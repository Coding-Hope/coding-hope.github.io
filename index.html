What is Coding Hope?

Coding Hope is a movement that provides local organizations affected by the COVID-19 pandemic with the personalized tech tools to support their impact and legacies in an online space.

Coding Hope invites developers and aspiring developers to join the movement to help our local businesses and nonprofits to continue having an impact throughout the pandemic.

If you'd like to learn more and join, please reach out to us and consult our Coding Hope brochure (click here).
